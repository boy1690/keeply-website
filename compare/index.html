What is Keeply, exactly?

A Windows and macOS desktop app that quietly tracks every save of your project files — Blender, PSD, Office docs, PDFs, images. No Git knowledge required; no per-seat licensing. Your files stay on your NAS (or local disk) — Keeply just handles the version history.
