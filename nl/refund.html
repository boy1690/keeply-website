1. Merchant of Record

All purchases are processed by Paddle.com, which acts as the Merchant of Record for all Keeply orders. Paddle is responsible for payment processing, invoicing, tax calculation, and billing-related customer support.

When you purchase a Keeply license, you are transacting with Paddle, not directly with Keeply. Your purchase is also subject to Paddle's Buyer Terms and Paddle's own refund policies.

2. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee (Team Plan)

Keeply's Team Plan (monthly and annual subscriptions) comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee.

If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, contact us within 30 days of your initial purchase date and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

To request a refund:

  • Email hello@keeply.work with your order details, or
  • Contact Paddle directly at paddle.com/contact

3. Early Access Perpetual License

The Early Access Perpetual License ($599) is eligible for a refund within 30 days of purchase, provided the license key has not been activated or used.

After 30 days, perpetual license purchases are non-refundable.

4. After the 30-Day Window

After 30 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis in accordance with:

  • Paddle's Refund Policy
  • Applicable consumer protection laws in your jurisdiction

Statutory Rights

  • EU / EEA / UK residents: You may have a statutory 14-day right of withdrawal for digital products, subject to applicable conditions.
  • Taiwan residents: You may have rights under Taiwan's Consumer Protection Act (消費者保護法), including a potential 7-day inspection period.

These statutory rights are not limited by this policy.

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Subscription fees for periods already used beyond the 30-day window
  • Renewals (monthly or annual) after the renewal has processed
  • Perpetual licenses purchased more than 30 days ago

6. How to Request a Refund

Option 1 — Contact Keeply:

Email hello@keeply.work with:

  • The reason for your refund request

Option 2 — Contact Paddle directly:

Visit paddle.com/contact or use the receipt email you received from Paddle.

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days.

7. Refund Processing

Refunds are processed by Paddle and typically appear within 5–10 business days, depending on your payment method and financial institution.
